Claim one tracked Chimera obligation.

Use this tool when you are taking responsibility for reviewing or updating a specific obligation.

Do NOT use this tool to create obligations or choose from candidates. Use `chimera_obligations_sync` first when findings need tracking.

Parameter rules:
- Prefer `ref` copied from `Ref: obligation:<id>` output.
- `obligationID` remains accepted as a legacy raw obligation id.

Common calls:
- Claim a pending obligation before editing or reviewing its target.

After this tool:
- Review or update the target, then resolve or ignore the obligation with a reason.
