Ignore one tracked Chimera obligation with a reason.

Use this tool when an active obligation is intentionally not pursued because it is irrelevant, out of scope, duplicate, stale, or otherwise non-actionable.

Do NOT use this tool for obligations you reviewed and completed. Use `chimera_obligation_resolve` for completed work.

Parameter rules:
- Prefer `ref` copied from `Ref: obligation:<id>` output.
- `obligationID` remains accepted as a legacy raw obligation id.
- `reason` is required.
- `note` is optional supporting context.

Common calls:
- Ignore a duplicate, out-of-scope, or false-positive obligation with a short reason.

After this tool:
- Mention skipped obligations and reasons when they matter for final closeout.
