Resolve one tracked Chimera obligation.

Use this tool when the required review or update has been completed for a specific obligation.

Do NOT use this tool for skipped or irrelevant obligations. Use `chimera_obligation_ignore` with a reason instead.

Parameter rules:
- Prefer `ref` copied from `Ref: obligation:<id>` output.
- `obligationID` remains accepted as a legacy raw obligation id.
- `note` is optional and should briefly state the evidence or update that resolved it.

Common calls:
- Resolve an obligation after reviewing the target and applying any needed update.

After this tool:
- Continue until all relevant active obligations are resolved or explicitly ignored.
